# Campaign 4: Founder Story Update -- "Day 30+"
## "1 person. 0 code. A full business platform."

### Target Audience
- Indie hackers, solo founders, tech enthusiasts
- AI/startup community (X, Reddit, LinkedIn, Product Hunt)

### The Story (updated)
Ramon (Zito), a Brazilian with zero coding skills, used Claude AI to build Touch? -- a proximity-based social network with ultrasonic connection, digital tipping, restaurant ordering, real-time delivery tracking, 5 operational modules, voice agents, and white-label sites for businesses. All from Brazil, targeting the US market. No investors. No team. Just one person and AI.

### What's new since "20 days":
- White-label restaurant ordering sites
- Full delivery system with real-time tracking
- Guest accounts (progressive registration)
- Google login integration
- Stripe payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, cards)
- 5 business modules (Restaurant, Parking, Gym, Barber, Church)
- Voice Agent with 3D avatar
- Star economy gamification
- Digital gifts system
- 100+ commits in 30 days
