# PROMPTS KLING AI -- BATCH 2 (March 2026)
# Cinematic Video Prompts for Touch? Marketing
# Style: Black Mirror meets Apple commercial
# All dialogue in English -- always ends with "Touch?"
# Format: 9:16 vertical (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
# Duration: 5-10 seconds per clip

---

## NOTES FOR EVERY PROMPT:
- Camera work: specify lens, movement, speed, angle
- Lighting: cinematic, moody, dramatic
- Dialogue: always in English, always the word "Touch?" spoken as a question
- The other person confirms with a nod, smile, or "Yeah" / "Let's go"
- Phones positioned SCREEN-TO-SCREEN (telas viradas uma pra outra), held vertically
- Backs of phones face outward (toward camera/viewer)
- Speakers at the bottom
- Vibe: Black Mirror S4E4 "Hang the DJ" -- technology that connects humans, slightly eerie but beautiful
- Color grade: teal and orange, desaturated shadows, warm highlights

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Color Grading Reference:
- Black Mirror S4E4 "Hang the DJ" -- warm/cold contrast, slightly desaturated
- Blade Runner 2049 -- amber, teal, purple haze
- Michael Mann's "Collateral" -- nighttime LA, sodium vapor
- Apple "Shot on iPhone" -- clean, intimate, natural

### Sound Design:
- Every "Touch?" moment has the same subtle sound signature: a low-freq hum + soft chime
- Build a recognizable audio brand around this sound
- Ambient sounds are always present (bar noise, traffic, music) but fade during the touch moment
- The touch moment exists in its own sonic space -- quieter, more intimate

### The "Touch?" Habit:
- In EVERY video, someone asks "Touch?" as a question
- The other person always confirms (verbally or with a gesture)
- This trains viewers to associate the word and gesture
- Like "Hey Siri" or "OK Google" but for human connection

### Phone Position (CRITICAL):
- Always held vertically
- Screens facing each other (screen-to-screen)
- Backs of phones face the camera/viewer
- Speakers at the bottom
- The gesture should look natural, like a fist bump but with phones
- Never top-to-top, never back-to-back

### Kling AI Settings:
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- Duration: 5-10 seconds per clip
- Style: Cinematic, photorealistic
- Negative prompt: cartoon, anime, illustration, low quality, blurry, watermark
- Model: Use highest quality available
- Multiple clips can be stitched together in editing software
