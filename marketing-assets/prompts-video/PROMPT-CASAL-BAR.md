# PROMPT VIDEO -- Casal no Bar (Kling AI / Veo 3 / Runway)

## Conceito
Casal se paquera de longe num bar, ninguem tem coragem de chegar.
Ele mostra a tela do Touch? pra ela, ela mostra de volta.
Ele passa rapido pela mesa dela, encostam os celulares (Touch!), e se despedem.
Mas continuam conversando pelo app, sorrindo cada um na sua mesa.

## Formato
- 15 segundos, 9:16 vertical (Stories/Reels/TikTok)
- Estilo cinematico, iluminacao quente de bar, leve bokeh
- SEM dialogo falado -- so musica ambiente e som sutil do Touch

---

## PROMPT (copiar e colar)

```
Cinematic vertical 9:16 video, 15 seconds, warm bar lighting with soft amber tones and bokeh.

SCENE 1 (0s-3s): A stylish young woman sits alone at a bar table, glancing across the room. Reverse angle shows a young man at another table catching her eye. They exchange shy smiles but neither moves. Warm overhead pendant lights, exposed brick wall, casual upscale bar.

SCENE 2 (3s-6s): Close-up of the man's hands holding his phone toward her direction, screen facing her. The phone screen shows the REFERENCE IMAGE (the Touch? app constellation screen with connected profile nodes on dark background). She sees it and smiles. Cut to her doing the same -- holding her phone screen toward him showing the same app.

SCENE 3 (6s-9s): He stands up casually, walks past her table. Quick smooth motion -- they press their phones together screen-to-screen for one second. A subtle warm glow pulses between the phones. He keeps walking, she watches him go with a soft smile.

SCENE 4 (9s-13s): Back at their separate tables. Both looking down at their phones, typing and smiling. She laughs softly reading something on her screen. He grins. The distance between them feels electric now, not awkward.

SCENE 5 (13s-15s): Wide shot of the whole bar. Two strangers at separate tables, both glowing from their phone screens, connected. White text fades in: "Touch?" -- then below it: "touch-irl.com"

Style: Warm color grade, shallow depth of field, handheld micro-movements for intimacy. No dialogue. Soft indie acoustic guitar background. Lens flares from bar lights.
```

## IMAGEM DE REFERENCIA

Usar o screenshot da tela "MINHA REDE / MY NETWORK" como a tela do celular que aparece no video.
A tela mostra a constelacao com nos conectados (perfis) no fundo escuro.

**PRIVACIDADE**: Os nomes e fotos nos nos da constelacao devem ser ficticios.
