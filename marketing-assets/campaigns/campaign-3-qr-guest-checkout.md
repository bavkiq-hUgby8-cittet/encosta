# Campaign 3: QR Code + Guest Checkout
## "Scan. Order. Done. No app. No signup."

### Target Audience
- Consumers (demand side) -- people who eat out, order delivery, go to events
- Age 18-35, US metros
- Tech-savvy, hate downloading apps, value speed

### Pain Points We Solve
1. "Download our app" fatigue (avg person uses 9 apps/day, won't download another)
2. Forced account creation just to order food
3. Slow checkout flows with too many fields
4. No visibility into delivery status at small restaurants

### Value Proposition
Touch? lets you scan a QR code, browse the menu, and pay with Apple Pay -- all in 15 seconds, right in your browser. No app. No account. No friction.

## Ad Creatives

### Creative 1: "15 seconds. That's it."
- **Visual**: Timer counting from 0 to 15 with each step appearing -- Scan QR (3s), Pick food (7s), Apple Pay (5s) = Order placed
- **Headline**: 15 seconds. That's it.
- **Body**: Scan. Pick. Pay. Your order is in. No app download. No account needed. Just your phone and Apple Pay.
- **Format**: 9:16 video (TikTok/Reels)

### Creative 2: "Stop downloading restaurant apps."
- **Visual**: Phone screen full of food delivery app icons, then all get deleted. Touch? browser tab stays.
- **Headline**: Stop downloading restaurant apps.
- **Body**: Touch? runs in your browser. Scan the QR code at any Touch? restaurant and order instantly. Works everywhere.
- **Format**: 9:16 + 1:1

### Creative 3: "Track your food. In real time."
- **Visual**: Split screen -- text message saying "your food is on the way" vs Touch? live tracking map with delivery moving
- **Headline**: Track your food. In real time.
- **Body**: See exactly where your delivery is. No guessing. No refreshing. Live tracking, straight from the restaurant to your door.
- **Format**: 9:16
