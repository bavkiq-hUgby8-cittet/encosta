## POSTING STRATEGY

### Platform breakdown
- **TikTok** (60% budget): Primary acquisition channel. Short, punchy, native feel.
- **Instagram Reels** (30% budget): Brand building + retargeting. Polished aesthetic.
- **YouTube Shorts** (10% budget): SEO long-tail. Evergreen discovery.

### Content pillars (rotate weekly)
1. **"Touch moment"** -- Raw clips showing the gesture (emotional hook)
2. **"How it works"** -- Quick explainer overlays on video clips
3. **"Use case spotlight"** -- B2B focused (tipping, check-in, parking)
4. **"Real connections"** -- Social moments (airport, campus, concert)

## PAID TRAFFIC STRATEGY -- US MARKET

### Phase 1: Awareness (Week 1-2) -- $20-30/day
**Objective:** Video views + profile visits
**Audiences:**
- US, 18-35, interest in: contactless payments, nightlife, fitness, food trucks, live events
- Lookalike: website visitors (install Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel first!)

**Best videos for ads:**
1. bar-tipping (8s) -- short, visual, instantly understandable
2. campus-connection (5s) -- ultra-short, emotional, scroll-stopping
3. parking-garage (15s) -- clear B2B value prop

**TikTok Spark Ads format:**
- Post organically first, then boost the best performers
- Use Spark Ads (boosting organic posts) -- 2-3x better CTR than regular ads
- Test 3-4 videos, kill underperformers after 48h

**Instagram:**
- Reels ads with "Learn More" CTA to touch-irl.com
- Stories ads with the 5s campus video (perfect length for Stories)

### Phase 2: Consideration (Week 3-4) -- $30-50/day
**Objective:** Website traffic + time on site
**Audiences:**
- Retarget video viewers (watched 50%+)
- Interest expansion: hospitality workers, event planners, small business owners
- US cities with high tipping culture: NYC, LA, Miami, Austin, Nashville, Chicago

**Ad creative rotation:**
- Video 1: B2B angle (parking/restaurant) + text overlay "No POS. No app. Just a speaker."
- Video 2: Social angle (airport/rooftop) + text overlay "The app that only works IRL"
- Video 3: Founder story angle + text overlay "1 person + AI = payment app in 20 days"

### Phase 3: Conversion (Week 5+) -- $50-100/day
**Objective:** Waitlist signups / early adopters
**Audiences:**
- Retarget site visitors (spent 30s+)
- Lookalike from waitlist signups
- Small business owner targeting
