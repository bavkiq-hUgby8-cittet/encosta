Frequently asked questions
Is there a monthly fee?
No. Touch? is free to join. You only pay the gateway fee per transaction (shown in the table above) plus the 10% Touch? platform fee.
Can I use both Stripe and Mercado Pago?
Yes! We recommend connecting both. Your customers will see all available payment methods and choose the one they prefer.
When do I receive my money?
With Stripe, payouts arrive in 2 business days. With Mercado Pago, it varies between instant (PIX) and up to 14 days depending on the payment method and your account status.
What is the 10% Touch? fee?
Touch? charges a 10% platform fee on each transaction. This covers the app, support, analytics, and all the technology that connects you with your customers. The gateway fee (Stripe or MP) is separate and charged by the payment provider.
What about refunds and chargebacks?
Customers can request a refund within 24 hours. Operators can process refunds from their dashboard. Chargebacks follow each gateway's policy — Stripe charges $15 per dispute, while Mercado Pago varies by case.
