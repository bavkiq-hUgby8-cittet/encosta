# Campaign 2: White-Label Restaurant & Delivery
## "Your restaurant deserves its own app. Without building one."

### Target Audience
- Restaurant owners (small to mid-size)
- Food truck operators
- Bar owners
- Coffee shop owners
- Ghost kitchens / cloud kitchens
- Anyone paying monthly fees for delivery apps

### Pain Points We Solve
1. Third-party delivery apps take 15-30% commission
2. Building a custom ordering app costs $10K-50K+
3. Customers hate downloading yet another app
4. No real-time tracking without expensive integrations
5. POS hardware is expensive and clunky

### Value Proposition
Touch? gives any restaurant its own branded ordering website with Stripe payments, Apple Pay, Google Pay, real-time delivery tracking, and guest checkout -- all running in the browser, zero code, zero downloads.
